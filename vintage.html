Vintage Festival Project

For this project, I undertook the creation of a Vintage Festival concept, using various promotional materials to advertise the event. I developed promotional posters, designed commemorative tickets, and crafted informative brochures to announce the diverse offerings of the festival.

The Vintage Festival will serve as a vibrant celebration, showcasing a captivating catwalk featuring an array of vintage clothing styles. Moreover, the event will boast an extensive collection of popup shops and numerous vendors, offering attendees an experience filled with nostalgia and unique finds.

All designs and illustrations were made through Adobe Illustrator and Procreate.
