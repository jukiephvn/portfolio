The Problem & The Solution

In a world that is largely divided, fostering communities across diverse backgrounds has become increasingly challenging.

The introduction of Umomi aims to provide a platform for individuals to experience new cultures and, most importantly, an opportunity to come together. Umomi is an app that encourages cultures of community and inclusivity through the love of food. Our community forum allows users to share stories, breaking down barriers. Umomi is the perfect recipe for a more compassionate, connected, and all-embracing world.

How can a cultural recipe app reconnect college students with their cultural traditions? How do we promote cultural sensitivity?
