ENIGMA Website Revamp

As the designer spearheading the website revamp for ENIGMA - UCLA’s hub for board game enthusiasts, Dungeons & Dragons aficionados, and all things nerdy, I embarked on a mission to elevate their digital presence.

Given the project's scope and timeline, I adopted a strategic design approach, harnessing my creativity within a lean framework to deliver impactful visuals and a user-friendly interface. Drawing inspiration from the club's diverse interests, I translated their essence into a visually captivating and intuitive website.

By seamlessly merging aesthetic appeal with functionality, I aimed to create an immersive digital space that not only showcases the club's diverse activities but also fosters a sense of community among its members.
